Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta made in very wide, flat sheets. It is a traditional Italian dish that consists of stacked layers of pasta alternating with fillings such as ragù (ground meats and tomato sauce), béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses (which may include ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and seasonings and spices. The dish is typically topped with grated cheese, which melts during baking. Lasagna is usually served as a hot, oven-baked dish and is cut into single-serving square or rectangular portions.
