Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta made in very wide, flat sheets. It is a traditional Italian dish that consists of stacked layers of pasta alternating with fillings such as ragù (ground meats and tomato sauce), béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses (which may include ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and seasonings and spices. The dish is typically topped with grated cheese, which melts during baking. Lasagna is usually served as a hot, oven-baked dish and is cut into single-serving square or rectangular portions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles: 12 (uncooked)
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese: 4 cups (1 quart)
  • Shredded Parmesan Cheese: ½ cup (4 oz)
  • Salt: ¾ teaspoon
  • Ground Beef: ½ pound
  • Italian Sausage: ½ pound
  • Yellow Onion: 1 (diced)
  • Garlic: 2 cloves (minced)
  • Pasta Sauce: 36 ounces (1 kg)
  • Tomato Paste: 2 tablespoons
  • Italian Seasoning: 1 teaspoon
  • Ricotta Cheese: 2 cups (1 pint)
  • Parsley: ¼ cup (60 ml), chopped fresh
  • Egg: 1 large, beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the lasagna noodles and cook until al dente (firm) according to package directions. Drain, rinse under cold water, and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, brown the beef, sausage, onion, and garlic over medium-high heat until no pink remains. Drain any fat.
  4. Stir in the pasta sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, ½ teaspoon of salt, and ¼ teaspoon of black pepper. Simmer uncovered over medium heat for 5 minutes or until slightly thickened. Taste and season with additional salt if desired.
  5. In a separate medium bowl, combine 1 ½ cups mozzarella cheese, ¼ cup Parmesan cheese, ricotta, parsley, egg, and ¼ teaspoon salt.
  6. Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce in a 9x13 pan or casserole dish. Top it with 3 lasagna noodles. Layer with ⅓ of the ricotta cheese mixture and 1 cup of meat sauce. Repeat twice more. Finish with 3 noodles topped with remaining sauce.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil and sprinkle the top of the lasagna with the remaining 2 ½ cups mozzarella cheese and ¼ cup Parmesan cheese. Bake uncovered for an additional 15 minutes or until browned and bubbly. Broil for 2-3 minutes if desired.
  9. Rest for at least 15 minutes before cutting.
